About

Steve Dawson
Managing Director

Steve Dawson has 26+ years of experience in hotel and resort transactions across Australia and Asia. He has advised developers and investors on hotel positioning, branding and asset sales - including modular hotel developments and large resort accommodation assets.

His work includes the branding and sale of the Oaks Elan SOHO Darwin, one of Australia's largest modular hotel projects, sold to Minor Group Thailand. Steve has also previously worked with Mantra Group during their expansion phase prior to their acquisition by Accor, the world's largest hotel operator.

Steve has made it his mission to think outside the box and build solutions that optimise the capital value of resort accommodation properties - particularly modular hotels and strata-titled developments.

Case Study — Track Record

Oaks Elan SOHO Darwin

Darwin, Northern Territory — Australia

One of the largest modular hotel developments ever delivered in Australia. Steve Dawson played a key advisory role in the positioning, branding and sale of the asset — working alongside Gwello Developments from the early stages when the project was still a vacant site.

Steve assisted in aligning the project with the Oaks brand and facilitating the eventual acquisition of the completed hotel by Minor Group Thailand. The project demonstrated the commercial viability of modular hospitality development in Australia and established a successful precedent for future projects.
